About Us

TeamScrim is platform designed to offer various e-sports teams a chance to find competitive practice that they desire. We streamline all the information provided about the teams that register, for ease of communication. It is our goal to make finding practice easier than ever before for all levels of teams. No more reddit, twitter, or facebook posts and long tedious hours of communication.

What we offer?

Features

Simple and easy registration. Ability to create your team with detailed information about each player. Ability to post for a practice for a specific game. Allow you to choose which team to practice against. Messaging service to any team registered on the platform. Much more coming soon.
